Project Introductions
Name Description Umbrella Category
CyVerse / iPlant Develops and operates computational resources for grand challenges in the life sciences, including a petabyte-scale data store, Data Commons, and tools for sharing and preserving research data across the bioscience community. CyVerse / NSF Bioinformatics, Life Sciences Data
Neuronex A NeuroNex Technology Hub enabling enhanced-resolution 3D electron microscopy (3DEM) to discover new details about brain synapse function across different regions and species. NSF Bioinformatics, Neuroscience
UT System Research Cyberinfrastructure (UTRC) Provides comprehensive scientific computing capabilities across UT System institutions to attract faculty, students, and funding while enabling breakthrough research results. UT System Cyberinfrastructure, Research Computing
TACC User Portal A web interface for users to manage their TACC account, projects, and HPC allocations. TACC (Base Funded) Portals & Interfaces, User Services
DesignSafe A resource-sharing web platform for natural hazards research enabling simulations validated against real-world data, with curation/publication pipelines and a preservation back-end for engineering data. DesignSafe / NSF Natural Hazards Engineering, Data Management
Digital Rocks Portal A web-based portal for storage, management, and retrieval of images and experimental measurements of diverse porous materials, with customized metadata, analysis backend, and long-term preservation strategy. TACC Data Management, Geoscience / Porous Materials
Natural History Collections at TACC Maintains ongoing collaborations with the Texas Natural Sciences Center to digitize, host, and preserve natural history specimen collections. NSF / TACC Digital Collections, Natural History / Biodiversity
DrugDiscovery@TACC Enables high-throughput drug screening using Autodock Vina on TACC supercomputers, allowing researchers to search more than 700,000 drug-like compounds in only a few hours. TACC Health Informatics, Computational Drug Discovery
Chameleon A large-scale, reconfigurable experimental cloud testbed deployed at UC and TACC with 650 multi-core cloud nodes, 5 PB of disk space, and 100 Gbps inter-site connectivity for cloud systems research. NSF Research Computing Infrastructure, Cloud Testbed
Frontera TACC's flagship leadership-class supercomputer, providing computational capability for the largest and most complex science and engineering challenges across a wide spectrum of domains. NSF Research Computing Infrastructure, HPC Systems
Jetstream An interactive, on-demand cloud-based computational resource for the national research community, enabling scientists and engineers to conduct data analysis and computation anywhere, anytime. NSF Research Computing Infrastructure, Cloud / Interactive HPC
Accelerating Computing for Emerging Sciences (ACES) A composable computing platform removing bottlenecks in advanced computing by enabling flexible, as-needed aggregation of processors, accelerators, and memory components. NSF Research Computing Infrastructure, Next-Gen / Composable Computing
