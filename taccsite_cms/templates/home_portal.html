Core Experience Portal

The Texas Advanced Computing Center (TACC) provides powerful and intuitive web interfaces that remove technological hurdles.

The portal manages massive datasets, enables the execution of highly parallelized computations, and allows users to collaborate across the U.S. The portal is not just for scientists and engineers, TACC's resources can be used by technologists, industry partners, educators, humanities scholars, and others.

The portal's main features include:

Data Management

  • Upload from your device or cloud storage.
  • Preview many data formats.
  • Download anywhere.
  • Share with collaborators and reviewers.

Computation

  • Queue HPC Jobs.
  • Execute on some of the most powerful systems in the world.
