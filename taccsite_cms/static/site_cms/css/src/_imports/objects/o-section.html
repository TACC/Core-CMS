Section

I am a Section. Use light Sections to wrap most content. Use dark Sections to highlight some content.


Section (First of a Pair)

A light or dark Section directly below me will create a border between both. An un-styled Section never has a border.

Section (Second of a Pair)

A light or dark Section directly above me will create a border between both. An un-styled Section never has a border.


Section (with Nested Sections)

Sections can be nested within me. A nested Section removes the standard Section spacing.

(Nested) Dark Section

I am nested.

(Nested) Light Section

I am nested.


Section (at Bottom of Page)

At the bottom of a page: a dark Section will visually merge with the Footer, a light section will stand out above the Footer.
