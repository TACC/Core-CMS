Section

I am a Section. Use light Sections to wrap most content. Use muted Sections to gently highlight some content. Use dark Sections to strongly highlight some content.

Section (First of a Pair)

A light or muted or dark Section directly below me will create a border between both. An un-styled Section never has a border.

Section (Second of a Pair)

A light or muted or dark Section directly above me will create a border between both. An un-styled Section never has a border.

Section (before Muted Section)

A pair of light or dark Sections may have a muted Section between them.

(Muted) Section

A muted Section would appear between two light or dark Sections. It probably would not appear between two muted Sections.

Section (before Muted Section)

A pair of light or dark Sections may have a muted Section between them.
