Section

I am a Section. Use light Sections to wrap most content. Use dark Sectiosn to highlight some content.

Section (First of a Pair)

A Section directly below me will create a border between this Section and that Section.

Section (Second of a Pair)

A Section directly above me will create a border between this Section and that Section.

Section (with Nested Sections)

A dark Section and light Section can be nested within me.

A nested Section could be used to remove the standard Section spacing, but only as a way to distinguish a nested Section. (Nested Sections may not be something we want.)
